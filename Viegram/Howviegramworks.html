Viegram is a point driven photo-sharing app that allows you to share photos, earn points interacting, gain popularity and compete against friends in climbing a social status hierarchy.

It is an innovative photo-gaming experience where you receive points for posting photos, receiving likes, comments and more, thereby working your way to a greater social status and having your profile displayed on users devices all over the world.

Viegram's social status hierarchy consists of 4 levels:

  1. NeophyteA user who has from 0 to 24,999 points
  2. BonafideA user who has from 25,000 to 99,999 points
  3. SocialiteA user who has from 100,000 to 999,999 points
  4. IconA user who has above 1 million points
